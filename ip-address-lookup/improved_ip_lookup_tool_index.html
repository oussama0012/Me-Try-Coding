Wait, What's an IP Address Again?

Think of your IP like your home address… but for the internet. It tells websites where to send all that juicy stuff — cat videos, memes, emails from your boss, etc.

Two kinds exist:

  • IPv4 – the classic (looks like 123.456.7.89)
  • IPv6 – the longer, fancier one (looks like it came from the future)

Every phone, laptop, or smart toaster has an IP. Even your grandma's tablet.
